# Left Hand Levels List
welcome to the Geometry Dash Left Hand Levels List!

rules:
1. the levels can only be beaten in 60-360FPS.
2. the level has to be a rated level, challenge, or a verified level
3. cannot be an impossible level!
4. you cannot play/beat the level if you usually play with your left hand
5. you must have a handcam and cheat indicator!

point system:
1. beating/verifying a top 1 gives 450 points
2. levels are dethroned by 25 points
3. if the top 1 is beaten with a coin route that is easier than the regular route, it makes the top 2 427 points.
4. if the top 1 is beaten with a coin route that is harder than the regular route, it makes the top 2 423 points.
5. coin points can stack, even if theres both easy and hard routes
